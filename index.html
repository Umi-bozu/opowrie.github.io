04 CubeSat Payload Frame ANU Rocketry · 2024 +

Designed and validated a lightweight CubeSat payload structure for integration into a rocket payload bay — optimised for minimum mass while meeting tight dimensional constraints.

Validated via CAD, machining, and ANSYS FEA. Passed fit and load acceptance testing on first attempt. Also identified a critical integration issue during documentation — the frame did not fit within the vehicle structure — and coordinated the corrective redesign that enabled the payload to fly.
